Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What Materials are your bottles made from?

A: Our bottles are crafted from high-quality materials such as glass, plastic, and eco-friendly options. Each material is chosen to best suit the product it will contain.

Q: Are your bottles reusable?

A: Yes, our bottles are designed to be durable and reusable. Simply wash and refill them as needed.

Q: Can I order custom bottle designs?

A: Absolutely! We offer customization options for bottle designs, including color, logo placement, and cap styles. Please contact us for more details on custom orders.

Q: Do you offer wholesale pricing?

A: Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please reach out to our sales team for more information on bulk order discounts.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We accept returns on unused and unopened bottles within 30 days of purchase. Custom orders are non-refundable. Please contact our customer service team for return instructions.
